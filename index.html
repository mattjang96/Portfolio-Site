About Me

My name is Matthew, and I am a Software Engineer. Currently, I am an engineer at The Home Depot, maintaining all of our product data, which feeds homedepot.com, supports the supply chain initiatives, and drives the SKU setup process for merchandising.

I am a critical thinker, and problem solver with deep interest in behavior, software development, and how we can better shape the human experience. I have experience in Java and C++, as well as in building dynamic web applications in JS, React, and Redux. I love the battle of developing an idea into existence, and forging the bridge between conceptualization and a tangible, impactful product. Programming has been my outlet for problem-solving and creative expression, and I am constantly sharpening my skills everyday. Great to meet you!
